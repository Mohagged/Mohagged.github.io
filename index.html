Currently working on:

"Dynamo" (working title) is a first person parkour platformer and speedrunning game. Imagine Sonic meets Mirror's Edge.

While the game was inspired by Warsows' movement system, a lot of influence stems from traditional 2D platforming games such as Sonic or Mario.
